About Us

Alex & Asher is more than just wrenches and water filtration. Jordon Alexander Glover and Jarod Asher Glover, brothers, created an avenue to not only flex their respective skills, but also to be a source of change they wanted to see within their business and personal communities, thus Alex & Asher was born.

Alex & Asher is a maintenance and repair company focused on future-forward solutions, ease and clarity of communication, and well-trained personnel. From designing comprehensive preventative maintenance plans molded to client needs to offering training classes to help shop owners become more familiar with their equipment. Functional and reliable equipment is the keystone to any successful food and beverage shop. Alex & Asher is here to ensure specialty shops receive special attention.

Our mission is to create a supportive foundation for the North Carolina specialty food and beverage shop via extensive client communication and well-trained attentive staff.
